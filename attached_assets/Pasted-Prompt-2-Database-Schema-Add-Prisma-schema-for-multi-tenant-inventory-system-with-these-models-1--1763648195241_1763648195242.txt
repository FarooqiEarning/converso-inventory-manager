2. User (with role: OWNER, MANAGER, CASHIER)
3. Store (physical outlets)
4. Product (with SKU, name, cost_price, sell_price)
5. InventoryItem (stock per product per store)
6. Customer (for credit tracking)
7. Sale (header with total, discount, payment_type)
8. SaleItem (line items)
9. Credit (udhar accounts)

- Proper relations
